📊 Understanding Your SEO Score

What's My Score?

Your SEO score is 0-100, with a letter grade (A+ down to F). Higher = better SEO health.

Score Range Grade What It Means
90-100 A+, A Excellent SEO — likely ranking well
80-89 B+, B Good SEO — some improvements possible
70-79 C+, C Fair SEO — multiple issues to fix
60-69 D+, D Poor SEO — significant problems
Below 60 F Critical issues affecting rankings

What Are Issues?

Issues are SEO problems that hurt your rankings. They're labeled by severity:

🔴 Critical — High impact. Fix immediately. (e.g., Missing H1 tag)

🟡 Warning — Medium impact. Fix soon. (e.g., Slow page speed)

⚪ Info — Low impact. Nice to have. (e.g., Missing Open Graph tags)

How Do I Fix These Issues?

Each issue comes with a suggestion. For example:

Issue: Missing H1 tag

Why it matters: Search engines use H1 to understand your page topic and improve rankings

Fix: Add an H1 tag near the top of your page with your main keyword

💡 Not all issues are equal: Fix the Critical ones first, then Warnings. Info items are bonus optimizations.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does Naraseo analyze? ▼

We check:

  • On-page SEO: Title, meta description, H1 tags, headings, content quality
  • Technical SEO: Mobile friendliness, page speed, structured data, canonical tags
  • Speed Metrics: Load time, responsiveness, visual stability analysis
  • Keywords: What you're ranking for, what you're missing
  • Backlinks: Domain authority (compared to competitors)
How long does an audit take? ▼

Usually 5-10 seconds per page. If your site is slow, it might take up to 30 seconds. This is normal.

Can I audit my competitors? ▼

Yes! You can audit any public website. This is a great way to see what they're doing better and find gaps in your own SEO strategy.

What if I exceed my daily limit? ▼

Your limit resets at midnight (UTC). If you need more audits before then, upgrade to Pro or Agency. You'll get instant access to higher limits.

Are my results saved? ▼

Yes. Your audit history is saved in your dashboard. You can compare audits over time to track your progress.

Can I export reports? ▼

Yes. Every audit can be exported as a professional PDF report. Great for sharing with your team or clients.

Is my data private? ▼

Yes. Your audit data is private. We don't sell your data or share it with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I cancel my subscription? ▼

Go to your Dashboard → Subscription → Click "Cancel Plan". You'll have access until your billing period ends.

Do I need technical knowledge to use this? ▼

No. Naraseo is built for everyone — from beginners to SEO pros. Our reports explain issues in plain English with actionable fixes.

What if my site isn't accessible online yet? ▼

Naraseo needs to reach your site publicly. If your site is behind a firewall or password-protected, we can't analyze it. Launch it to the internet first.
