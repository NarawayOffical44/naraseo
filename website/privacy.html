Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Naraseo AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account data: Name and email address when you create an account.
  • Usage data: URLs you audit, audit scores, and API usage counts — stored to provide audit history and enforce plan limits.
  • Technical data: Browser type, extension version, and error logs for debugging.
  • Contact submissions: Name, email, and message if you use our contact form.

We do not collect passwords in plain text, payment card details, or personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Naraseo AI service
  • To enforce plan limits and track API usage
  • To respond to support requests
  • To send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset)

We do not sell your data to third parties or use it for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted on AWS infrastructure in the US. Audit data is retained for 12 months and then deleted. You can request earlier deletion at any time.

4. Chrome Extension

The Naraseo AI Chrome Extension:

  • Reads page content (title, meta tags, headings, links) only on pages you explicitly audit
  • Sends page URL and extracted SEO data to our backend for analysis
  • Stores audit results and chat history locally in chrome.storage.local
  • Does not run in the background on pages you have not opened
  • Does not access passwords, form inputs, or financial data

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google PageSpeed Insights API — used to fetch Core Web Vitals; your URL is sent to Google
  • OpenPageRank — used for domain authority scores; your domain is sent to their API
  • Anthropic Claude API — used to power AI chat; page SEO context is sent to Anthropic
  • Supabase — database and authentication provider

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your audit history

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@naraseo.ai.

7. Cookies

The Naraseo AI website uses only session cookies for authentication. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify registered users by email of any material changes. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests: support@naraseo.ai
